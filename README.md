## ðŸŽ¨ UI/UX Design Philosophy
Our design language, **"Emotional Tech,"** aims to make technology feel less like a machine and more like a companion.

### 1. Emotional Palette
*   **Soft Pink (#FFB6C1) & Warm White (#FFF5F7)**: Departing from the cold, dark aesthetics of traditional "tech" apps, we use a soft, blooming palette that evokes comfort and friendliness.
*   **Hot Pink (#FF69B4)**: Used sparingly for actions and highlights to provide a vibrant, energetic pulse.

### 2. Mobile-First & Fluid responsiveness
*   **Unified Experience**: Whether on a desktop or a smartphone, the experience remains consistent.
*   **Tactile Interactions**: Buttons and cards respond with subtle animations, giving a sense of physical touch and responsiveness.
*   **Accessible Layouts**: Critical controls are placed within the "thumb zone" for effortless mobile navigation.

### 3. Futuristic Minimalism
*   **Glassmorphism & Soft Shadows**: We use translucent layers and soft ambient shadows to create depth without clutter.
*   **Typography**: Clean, geometric fonts (Montserrat, Noto Sans KR) prioritize readability while maintaining a modern edge.
*   **Immersive Modals**: Settings and menus appear as clean, floating cards rather than intrusive full-screen blockades.
